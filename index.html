Star Citizen Faction Ship Drawer

This tool uses HTML5 LocalStorage to store your inputs for future use.

You may also see clipping issues if you've scrolled during the rendering of the image, or are not at the top of the page when you click "Draw my Faction!". This is due to a the html2canvas plugin we're using, and is a known issue with that library.

Questions? Comments? Issues? Message /u/smdaegan on reddit or open an issue on GitHub!
